The Library

A small shelf of favourite books, retold in simple English and published a few chapters at a time. Think of it as a library: browse it, or don’t — the daily stories carry on either way.

Every book starts with a short note about what it is. New chapters are always added to the same page, so your place is easy to find — just scroll on from where you stopped.

  1. The Scholar’s Daughters — after Jane Austen — two sisters in old Korea · chapters 1–4
  2. The New Headmaster — after Anthony Trollope — a comedy about a school in old Korea · chapters 1–4
  3. The Small House at Allington — Trollope — two sisters in Victorian England · chapter 1
  4. Brigadier Gerard — Conan Doyle — the comic adventures of Napoleon’s vainest soldier · chapter 1
